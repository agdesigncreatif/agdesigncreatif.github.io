    How it started:

    My journey to soutache jewelry started 2 years ago when my parents came to visit me in Toronto. Just a short info I was born in Belarus but came to live in Canada 7 years ago with my husband and 2 sons. So my mom, knowing my creative heart, as I like to learn to do new things, brought me a book by Anneta Valious in Russian. I loved all the designs and I decided to give it a try. At first it seemed too hard to do but later I slowly discovered better quality materials to work with. If you wish to contact me, my information can be found below.
